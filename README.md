# Biophotons in Myelin Sheaths: Research Program

Statistical modeling, mathematical frameworks, and computational research into ultra-weak photon emissions in myelinated neural tissue.

## Research Tracks

| # | Track | Focus | Status |
|---|-------|-------|--------|
| 1 | [Photocount Statistics](tracks/01-photocount-statistics.md) | Rigorous reanalysis of biophoton count distributions using quantum optics protocols | Planned |
| 2 | [Time-Series & Fractal Analysis](tracks/02-time-series-fractal.md) | Temporal structure, memory, and self-similarity in emission sequences | Planned |
| 3 | [Waveguide Propagation Modeling](tracks/03-waveguide-propagation.md) | FDTD, ARROW, and multilayer transfer matrix models of myelinated axons | Planned |
| 4 | [Quantum Optics Formalism](tracks/04-quantum-optics.md) | Cavity QED, entangled biphoton generation, squeezed states in myelin | Planned |
| 5 | [Signal-to-Noise & Detection Theory](tracks/05-signal-to-noise.md) | Statistical inference at ultra-low photon rates, detector modeling | Planned |
| 6 | [Demyelination & Pathology](tracks/06-demyelination-pathology.md) | Testable predictions for MS and other demyelinating conditions | Planned |
| 7 | [Unified Multi-Scale Model](tracks/07-unified-model.md) | Connecting molecular generation -> waveguide transport -> network computation | Planned |
