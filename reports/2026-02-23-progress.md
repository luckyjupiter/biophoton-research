## Key Accomplishments

### 1. Physics-Based Waveguide Model ✅
**File**: `models/waveguide_physics.py`

- **Results**:

### 2. Critical Detection Analysis ✅
**File**: `models/detection.py`
- Implemented detector quantum efficiency curves
- **Major finding**: Standard detectors miss most human brain biophotons!
  - Human brain peak: 865nm (Wang et al. 2016)
  - Si PMT: captures only 8.7% (missing 91%)
  - EMCCD: captures only 42.2% (missing 58%)
  - InGaAs required for proper detection
- **Good news**: Cuprizone blueshift IMPROVES detection (+3.7%)
