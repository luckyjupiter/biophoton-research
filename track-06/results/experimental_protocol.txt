======================================================================
EXPERIMENTAL PROTOCOL: Biophoton Detection in Demyelinating Disease
Track 06 -- Demyelination & Pathology
======================================================================

OBJECTIVE: Test that demyelination alters biophoton emission
from neural tissue using established animal models.

HYPOTHESIS: Demyelinated nerve emits biophotons with (a) spectral
blueshift, (b) increased intensity during inflammation, (c) reduced
photon-photon correlations vs healthy myelinated tissue.

EXPERIMENT 1: EAE Mouse Model (Optic Nerve, Ex Vivo)
-------------------------------------------------------
MOG35-55-induced EAE in C57BL/6, n=8/timepoint x 3 groups x 5 = 120

  Time point                     Emission   Fold  SNR(1h)   t(SNR=5)
  --------------------------------------------------------------------
  Baseline (day 0)                   10.0   1.0x    175.2         3s
  Pre-symptomatic (day 7)           111.0  11.1x    598.2         0s
  Onset (day 10-12)                 515.0  51.5x   1291.0         0s
  Peak (day 14-18)                  927.5  92.8x   1733.0         0s
  Chronic (day 28+)                 172.5  17.2x    746.4         0s

EXPERIMENT 2: Cuprizone (Corpus Callosum, Longitudinal)
-------------------------------------------------------
0.2% cuprizone 6 weeks then normal chow, n=12/group x 2 = 24

  Time point                       Emission         Fold  SNR(1h)
  ----------------------------------------------------------------
  Week 0 (baseline)                    10.0         1.0x    175.2
  Week 1-2 (OL stress)                162.5        16.2x    724.4
  Week 3 (microglial)                 370.0        37.0x   1094.1
  Week 4-5 (active)                   537.5        53.8x   1319.0
  Week 6 (peak loss)                  352.5        35.2x   1067.8
  Week 8 (early remyel)               140.0        14.0x    672.2
  Week 12 (remyelinated)               72.5         7.2x    482.8

EXPERIMENT 3: LPC Focal Lesion (Sciatic Nerve, In Vivo)
-------------------------------------------------------
LPC injection, n=10, left sciatic LPC, right vehicle, days 3/7/14/21

DETECTION: EM-CCD (Hamamatsu ImagEM X2), PMT (H7421-40), SPADs (HBT)
CONTROLS: Dark counts, heat-killed, contralateral, TTX, NAC, sham
STATS: Two-way ANOVA, paired t-test, Bonferroni, Cohen's d
